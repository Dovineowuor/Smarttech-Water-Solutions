AquaLitech

IoT-Enabled Smart Water Meter and App

Track your water usage, detect leaks, monitor water quality, and get conservation tips with AquaLitech.

Features

  • Water Usage Tracking

    The AquaLitech app can track water usage for each user or location by recording water meter readings or using sensors.

  • Leak Detection

    The AquaLitech app can detect leaks and send alerts to the user by analyzing water usage patterns and detecting abnormal usage.

  • Water Quality Monitoring

    The AquaLitech app can monitor water quality and alert the user if there are any issues.

  • Water Conservation Tips

    The AquaLitech app provides tips and suggestions to reduce water usage and promote conservation.

  • Reporting and Analytics

    The AquaLitech app provides reports and analytics to help users understand their water usage patterns and identify areas for improvement.

About AquaLitech

AquaLitech is a smart water meter and app that helps users track their water usage, detect leaks, monitor water quality, and get conservation tips. Our mission is to help users save water and reduce their water bills.
